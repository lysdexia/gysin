gysin cut up editor

Gysin is a take on Brion Gysin's "cut-up" technique. A Surrealist method for creating interesting new combinations of words and images by simply cutting paper into strips and re-arranging them until something popped out and spanked one's limbic system.

This editor uses Markov Chains on a small database of pop-song lyrics to get a similar effect. This is not an "automatic songwriter" or any sort of artificial intelligence tool. Most of what comes out will be disjointed drivel. It's a fun toy that may help you with ideas, or maybe help you through a bit of writer's block. Using the output of this editor to produce actual pop music will probably not be noticed, though it's definitely what George Clinton was warning us about.
