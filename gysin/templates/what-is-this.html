gysin cut up editor

Gysin is a take on Brion Gysin's "cut-up" technique: a Surrealist method for creating interesting new combinations of words and images by simply cutting paper into strips and re-arranging them until something popped out and spanked one's limbic system.

This editor uses Markov Chains on a small database of pop-song lyrics (or epic poems, or fairy tales, it'll change from time to time) to get a similar effect.
